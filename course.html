G.N.M. (General Nursing and Midwifery)

The G.N.M. (General Nursing and Midwifery) program is a professional nursing course that prepares students for a successful career in e. The course combines theoretical learning with practical hospital training to develop skilled, compassionate, and ethical nursing professionals.

Duration 3 Years
Eligibility The candidate must have passed 10+2 (Higher Secondary) or equivalent from a recognized board in any stream (Science / Arts / Commerce).
