Shakti Institute of Nursing, Modasa

Shakti Institute of Nursing, Modasa is dedicated to providing quality education in the field of nursing and healthcare. The institute focuses on developing skilled, compassionate, and responsible nursing professionals who are committed to serving society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide excellent nursing education through academic learning, practical training, and ethical values so that students become competent healthcare professionals.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading institution in nursing education and contribute to the healthcare system by preparing professional and dedicated nurses.

Facilities

  • Well-equipped classrooms
  • Nursing laboratories
  • Clinical training in hospitals
  • Library and study resources
  • Safe and supportive campus environment
